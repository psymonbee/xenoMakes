🪙 Privacy & House Rules

This is a little game made for fun. Here's exactly what happens to your stuff, in plain words.

What we keep

  • Your nickname (please don't use your real name).
  • Your password — stored scrambled (hashed), so nobody, not even us, can read it.
  • The levels you make.

That's all. No email, no real names, no phone numbers. We don't ask for them and we don't want them.

Where it lives

Everything is saved on our own little server in one small file. We never sell it, share it, or show it to anyone else. There are no adverts and no trackers following you around.

Who can see your levels

  • "In progress" levels are private — only you can see them.
  • When you press Publish, a level moves to the Shared space so everyone can play it.

Deleting everything

Open the account button (top of the home page) and choose "Delete my account & all my levels". It's gone for good — your account and every level you made.

House rules 🤝

  • Be kind.
  • Never put your real name, address, school, or phone number in a nickname or a level name.
  • Make levels you'd be happy for a friend to play.

Questions? Ask the grown-up who set this up.
